Welcome to My Maximo Site

Here you can manage your assets and inventory.

Assets

Use our assets section to track and manage all of your company's physical assets. Our system allows you to:

  • Create and manage asset records
  • Track the location and status of assets
  • Generate reports on asset utilization and depreciation

Inventory

Use our inventory section to manage the stock levels and movement of your company's products and materials. Our system allows you to:

  • Create and manage inventory records
  • Track stock levels and replenishment needs
  • Generate reports on inventory turnover and cost of goods sold
